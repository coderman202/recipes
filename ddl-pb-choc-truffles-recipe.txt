Ingredients:
300g dark chocolate
300ml double cream
80g unsalted butter
3 tbsp dulce de leche
3 tbsp chunky peanut butter

Step 1: Melt butter in pan and slowly stir in double cream as it melts.
Step 2: When fully combined, bring off heat and stir in chocolate, broken into small pieces.
Step 3: When fully melted and combined, stir in dulce de leche and peanut butter until well mixed
Step 4: Place in container and refrigerate for at least 2 or 3 hours.
Step 5: Then take a small scooper and use hands to roll individual balls.
Step 6: Use cocoa powder to dust over truffles if you wish
